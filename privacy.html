Privacy Policy

How Aftercode protects data inside this live demo experience.

Aftercode keeps all user submissions private and never shares client materials beyond the intended project team. Content inside this demo is anonymized, cached locally, and reset regularly to prevent long-term storage of sensitive information.

Because this site is a public preview, no live customer accounts, payments, or personal data are processed. When the production platform launches, a full compliance review and signed agreements will govern every engagement.
