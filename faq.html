Frequently Asked Questions

Ten quick hits that cover how Aftercode ships builds, automation, and support in Demo Mode.

How does Demo Mode affect project timelines?

Demo Mode keeps code deploys and payment gateways paused, but the preview timeline reflects a real launch plan. Once we switch to production, milestones and assets follow the same cadence.

Can I see live examples of shipped work?

Yes — book a walkthrough and I'll share private staging links, Loom tours, and performance reports from recent launches.

What stacks do you use for websites and apps?

Most builds use modern JAMStack tooling, lightweight frameworks, and automation orchestration via JavaScript, Python, and no-code ops layers. We pick tools that match your team's comfort level.

Do you handle copywriting and brand messaging?

Absolutely. Every project includes optioned packages for copywriting, launch sequences, and brand guidelines so the visuals and words land together.

How do revisions work during a project?

You get async review windows with clear checkpoints. Feedback drops into the dashboard or your preferred tool, and we iterate inside the sprint.

What happens after launch?

We run a post-launch audit, hand off documentation, and outline support tiers for maintenance, analytics, and future feature drops.

Can you integrate my CRM or automation stack?

Yes. HubSpot, Airtable, Notion, Zapier, Make — we sync data flows so leads and operations stay in lockstep.

Do you offer custom PC or hardware builds?

Custom rigs are available on request. We scope performance requirements, source parts, assemble, and provide burn-in reporting.

How do we kick off a new project?

Send a quick brief through the contact form or Help Center. I'll schedule a roadmap call, confirm scope, and send a proposal with milestones.

Can we collaborate asynchronously?

Yes. Loom recaps, shared dashboards, and async standups are built into every engagement so you can stay on track without calendar overload.
